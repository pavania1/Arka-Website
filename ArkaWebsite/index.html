FAQs

We've collected the most commonly asked questions to help you find the answers you need.

What are you sovereign AI agents?
Sovereign AI agents are autonomous programs that operate independently on the blockchain without needing constant permission from central authorities. Think of them as self-running AI that makes its own decisions while staying secure and transparent.
What is the difference between AI agents vs sovereign Ai agents?
Regular AI agents need constant permission from central systems to operate. Sovereign AI agents work independently on the blockchain, making their own decisions and managing themselves.
What is multi-agent consensus engine?
It's a system that helps multiple AI agents work together and make decisions as a group, without needing a central authority.
What you can do with Arka Network?
With Arka, you can:

  • • Deploy them across different blockchains
  • • Keep your data private
  • • Make money from your AI creations
  • • Trade AI agents in our marketplace
